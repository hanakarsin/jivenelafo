How to check your KRA PIN using an ID number in a few simple steps

February 12, 2024
Post

A KRA PIN is a must-have for every Kenyan citizen who is 18 years old and above. If you forget your PIN, you can retrieve it via the KRA PIN checker using ID number. There are several other ways you can recover this number.

A KRA PIN helps the Kenya Revenue Authority, other Government agencies, and service providers like suppliers and creditors identify you or your company when doing business transactions. If you can't remember your PIN, recover it quickly to avoid further inconveniences. Learn how to retrieve the KRA PIN below:

How to use the KRA PIN checker using ID number in Kenya

The KRA iTax portal is also a PIN checker because it has a section that allows you to check your PIN. Suppose you have the email account and password you used during your iTax registration process:

  • Log into the iTax portal using your User ID number.
  • Hover the cursor on "Registration."

Other ways to check KRA PIN using ID number

There are other KRA PIN retrieval methods if you have your ID number. Discover how to check your KRA PIN using the ID number below.

Use the KRA M-Service app

Taxpayers can access their PINs and TTC, file tax returns, and register and pay for Turnover Tax (TOT) and Monthly Rental Income (MRI) via the KRA app using their ID numbers. You can download the KRA M-Service app to your gadget from Google PlayStore.

Contact KRA via social media

Sending a direct message to official KRA social media pages is a fast and convenient option. These pages are open to serve the public between Monday and Friday from 7 am to 9 pm.

Call KRA or send them a message via WhatsApp

You can send a message to KRA's WhatsApp channel. Alternatively, call 0711099999 and follow our self-service menu to retrieve your PIN. You should only call the number from 7 am to 9 pm, Monday to Friday, to request KRA PIN retrieval services.

Email KRA

KRA's official email address is callcenter@kra.go.ke. Request KRA PIN recovery assistance through that email and attach a colored scanned copy of your ID card. After that, make a follow-up through the nearest KRA office, Huduma Centre, or official KRA social media pages.

Visit a nearby KRA office or Huduma Centre

You can visit the nearest KRA office or Huduma Centre and ask for KRA PIN recovery services at the customer service desk. You will provide your original national ID card, after which you will receive a printed copy of the document. It is advisable to store your KRA document safely for future reference whenever you need to use your KRA PIN.

What do you do if you can't remember your KRA PIN?

If you cannot remember your KRA PIN (Personal Identification Number), check it on the KRA iTax PIN checker website or app.

Can you use the KRA PIN checker to retrieve your PIN?

The KRA iTax PIN checker app or website can help you retrieve your PIN if you need help remembering it and also confirm if your PIN is genuine.

You must have your ID number to use this service, and it is also important to note that the information generated by the KRA PIN checker system is limited to the basic details of the taxpayer.

How can you get your KRA PIN by SMS?

You cannot retrieve your KRA PIN via SMS.

How do you find your KRA PIN number using your ID number?

You can only retrieve your PIN using your ID number through the KRA iTax portal, KRA M-Service app, a KRA office, Huduma Centre, or contact KRA via 0711099999, callcenter@kra.go.ke, or official socials (WhatsApp channel, Twitter, Facebook, and Instagram).

How do you retrieve your KRA email?

If you forgot your email address, send a colored scanned copy of your ID card to callcentre@kra.go.ke for assistance. Alternatively, log into the KRA iTax portal using your PIN and password, then initiate changing an email address under "Registration- Amend PIN." Once the taxpayer initiates the process, a task is created for approval by a KRA officer.

How do you log into the KRA portal?

Go to the KRA iTax portal and enter your ID number or KRA PIN and password. After that, answer the Security Stamp mathematical question correctly and click "Log in."

How long does it take to get a KRA PIN online?

Applicants in Kenya receive their PIN Certificates instantly after completing the online registration form. Meanwhile, non-residents receive an acknowledgment receipt, which they should present to iTax (Railways Club), alongside other relevant documents, to complete their registration process.

Can you have two KRA PINs?

Only one KRA PIN is issued to a person, and it cannot be used by anyone other than the one to whom it was given to.

When should you get a KRA PIN?

The law requirers you to have a KRA PIN when making business transactions like buying and selling land, importing goods, registering a business, and many more.

How do you get a KRA PIN?

You get a KRA PIN after registering on the KRA iTax portal. You need the required documents and details to complete the process.

This is how you register for your KRA PIN:

  • Visit the KRA iTax portal.
  • Select "New PIN Registration."
  • Select "Non-Individual" and "Online Form" as your registration mode.
  • Fill in your basic information.
  • Select a suitable tax obligation by checking the relevant boxes.
  • Enter details of company directors or partners.
  • Fill in agent details if you have one.
  • Enter the result of the arithmetic sum provided and submit.

How can you check your TCC online?

You can now check if your Tax Compliance Certificate is valid on the KRA M-Service app. Download the app or log into the iTax portal and select the TCC checker.
