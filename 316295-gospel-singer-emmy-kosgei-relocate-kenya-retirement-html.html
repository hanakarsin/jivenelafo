Celebrated gospel singer Emmy Kosgei has said she will eventually return to Kenya years after relocating.

The beauty who is married to Nigerian preacher Ansel Madubuko has lived in Nigeria for six blissful years.

"Umh, 30 years from now I would like to have retired and will probably be watching the guys I educated all grown up. I will be in my 60s or 70s. I also want to move back to Kenya and enjoy my retirement," she said.

Emmy who also happens to be a pastor even went ahead to gush about her loving hubby.

Asked about how her husband describes her, the beauty had two distinct words "my Nubian queen."

According to the gospel icon, her husband appreciates her complexion and beauty and is always amazed by her strength.

"You know he always calls me his Nubian queen because Nubians are dark and beautiful," she said.

Emmy moved to Nigeria in 2013 after celebrating two elaborate wedding ceremonies.

The beauty pops in to Kenya from time to time for events or just to hang out with family and friends.
