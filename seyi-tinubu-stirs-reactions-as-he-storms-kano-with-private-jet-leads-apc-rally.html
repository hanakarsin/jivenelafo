Seyi Tinubu Stirs Reactions As He Storms Kano With Private Jet, Leads APC Rally

July 15, 2024
Post

Seyi, the son of the presidential candidate of the All Progressives Congress (APC), Bola Tinubu, has sparked mixed reactions online following a rally at Kano State.

Naija News reports that Seyi reportedly stormed Kano on Sunday with a private jet to lead a rally in support of his father for the forthcoming 2023 presidential election.

The director of the Tinubu/Shettima campaign support group, Baffa Babba Danagundi who spoke at the event, said the rally is aimed at sensitizing supporters of Tinubu to get their Permanent Voters Card, PVC.

The rally which was tagged ‘one million man PVC march,’ paralyzed commercial activities in the state as a mammoth crowd was seen following Seyi and his entourage.

This sparked mixed reactions from many Nigerians, while some commended Seyi and dared children of other presidential candidates to do likewise, opposition supporters frowned at it.
