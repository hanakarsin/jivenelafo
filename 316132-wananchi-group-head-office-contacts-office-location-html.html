Wananchi Group head office, contacts, office location

Wananchi Group Holdings (Wananchi) is a dominant East African internet and entertainment company with investments that span different sectors. Also, they are preeminent in the corporate communications industry. The name Wananchi Group was inherited from the former Wananchi online. At that time, the firm was dedicated to providing the growing African consumer market with first-class and affordable internet service for homes and businesses. The company’s modernistic approach in the current business environment is one of great diversity and unparalleled investment in technology. Wananchi Group boasts of a perfectly integrated media and telecoms service in various business divisions.

Established in 2008, Wananchi Group has massively grown its operations in different regions through its multifaceted services. They have Zuku Triple Play, Zuku DTH, Wananchi Programming Group, SimbaNet, Wananchi Telekom, and iSat. All of these services are both regional and local and the company is always putting more effort into expanding its foothold in the continent. Therefore, the firm has created a good foundation as a major service provider in telecommunications, TV, and internet both in the private and public sectors.

Wananchi Group services

The company offers internet speeds of up to 250Mbps that start from as low as 5, 10, 30, 50, and 100 and go up to its pedigree 250Mbps. In the Zuku Digital TV services, clients can easily access all popular free-to-air channels as well as a massive selection of other channels covering sports, entertainment, lifestyle and many more. Customers can easily add to their bouquet by subscribing to other premium channels.

Wananchi Group has positioned itself as a resourceful service provider in both satellite and fibre internet and digital television services. With Zuku TV, fibre, and telephone administrations (triple play), the firm has managed to grasp a sizeable market share and bring in massive profits.

Who owns Wananchi Group?

The Wananchi Group management has been shrouded in mystery as well as controversy; however, the writing is on the wall. The company’s currently funded by seven investors with Alice SA and Helios Investment Partners as the most recent. Currently, Wananchi Group possesses four team members. There are Njeri Rionge and Joseph Mucheru who are the co-founders. On the other hand, Thomas Hintze acts as the CEO and Joseph Muriithi as the COO (Chief Operating Officer).
