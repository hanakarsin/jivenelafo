Best university to study Architecture in Nigeria

May 15, 2024
Post

If you want to choose architecture as a career, you will definitely want to know what best university to study it in Nigeria. Luckily, you have us, as we are here to tell you about top 5 best Nigerian universities where you can study architecture. Come and check this out!

While we could have just told you about one great university you can attend to study Architecture in Nigeria, we have decided that it would not be enough. Besides, it is quite hard to determine which of the following universities is actually better than others. So we leave that part to you, as we list these universities with their entry requirements for Architecture. Universities are listed in no particular order.

University of Jos

  • Location: Jos, Plateau State
  • Founded in: 1975
  • Website: www.unijos.edu.ng

One of the top universities in the country that offer Architecture is the University of Jos. Their four-year Architecture programme is among the best in the country, and it is quite affordable, too. Here are the admission requirements:

Obafemi Awolowo University

  • Location: Ile-Ife, Osun-State
  • Founded in: 1961
  • Website: www.oauife.edu.ng

The Architecture programme at the Obafemi Awolowo University has existed since 1982 and has produced more than a thousand highly skilled graduates. If you are interested in enrolling, check out the admission requirements.

  • Direct Entry: You will need a General Certificate of Education (or the equivalent of GCE) with advanced level passes in Physics, Maths and another subject of your choice. You will also need an Ordinary or Higher National Diploma in Architecture with an upper credit. UTME requirements should be met as well.
  • UTME: 5 credits in your Senior School Certificate (or their equivalent) in Maths, English, Physics, as well as in two other subjects (Economics, Fine Arts/Technical Drawing, Biology, Geography, Agricultural Science or Chemistry).
  • UTME subjects: English and Maths (compulsory), as well as two other subjects among Chemistry, Geography, Economics, Physics or Technical Drawing.

Olabisi Onabanjo University

  • Location: Ago Iwoye, Ogun State
  • Founded in: 1982
  • Website: oouagoiwoye.edu.ng

Last but not least, we have Architecture programme from Olabisi Onabanjo University. If you want to study there, here are the requirements you should meet.

  • Direct Entry: You will need 2 A level passes, one of them being in Physics or Maths, and the other in Fine Arts, Chemistry or Geography. You will also need an Ordinary or Higher National Diploma in a relevant field with an upper credit.
  • UTME: You will need 5 SSCE credit passes in Maths, English, Chemistry, Physics, as well as in one other subject of your choice. See full list of possible subjects here.
  • UTME subjects: English (compulsory), Maths, Physics, as well as any subject among Art, Chemistry, Biology, Economics and Geography.
