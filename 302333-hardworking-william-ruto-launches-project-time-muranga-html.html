Hardworking William Ruto launches project ahead of time in Murang'a

July 13, 2024
Post

Deputy President William Ruto is known to be an energetic politician who can easily hop from one function to the other in a single day.

On Wednesday, April 3, the man from Sugoi village who has a record of commissioning projects across the country launched a classroom in Murang'a county ahead of schedule.

The DP was expected to launch the classroom at Kamahuha Girls High School in Maragua constituency on Thursday, April 4, but commissioned it on Wednesday.

"Investing in education infrastructure is key for an optimal learning environment; effective instruction of learners and a check on dropout rates. It promotes skills development, proficiency and readies the youth to drive the wheels of the economy," said Ruto.

Among other leaders who attended the function included Murang'a Governor Mwangi Wa Iria and other senior county officials.

However, despite his hard work in promoting education, some naughty Kenyans laughed off the number two in government saying he was desperate for publicity in launching a classroom ahead of schedule.
