Policemen 'Catch Fun' As Hoodlums Fight Dirty In Agbado Area Of Lagos

March 25, 2024
Post

There was a commotion in the Iyana Iba area of Lagos on Wednesday after hoodlums said to be from the Agbado area clashed.

The horrific fight, Naija News understands, happened before officers of the Nigeria Police Force who were seen fully dressed in their uniforms walking around the scene.

It is yet unclear what the genesis of the fight was, but the hoodlums could be seen in viral videos throwing stones and sharp objects at each other from distances while they move at range to attack the opponent group.

One of the police officers is captured on camera making a video of the events while other personnel move around watching the fight.

In one of the videos, an elderly man who was involved in throwing objects at the opponent group was taken down.

Some supporters of All Progressives Congress (APC) presidential candidate, Bola Tinubu, were spotted eating agbado (corn in Yoruba) at the party’s concert in Abuja on Wednesday.

Naija News reports that a concert was organised by one of Tinubu’s support groups, City Boy Movement, following the indefinite postponement of the inauguration of the APC presidential campaign council.

In the photos making the rounds online, some young men believed to be APC supporters were seen eating ‘Agbado’ at the Unity Fountain venue of the concert.
