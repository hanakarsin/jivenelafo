Loads of winners emerged at the much-anticipated 10th edition of the Nigeria Entertainment Awards (NEA) that went down on Sunday, September, 6.

The event that had many of the country’s top entertainers in attendance at the NYU Skirball Performing Center, New York, USA, was thrilling in many respects.

Chocolate City’s Vice President, Ice Prince Zamani, and Tinsel star Osas Ighodaro-Ajibade were the hosts of the awards. According to reports, the duo did a great job connecting perfectly on stage.

Winners from the 39 categories included a surprise win by DJ Cuppy, who beat Chocolate City’s DJ Lambo to the Best Female Jockey award.
