A comprehensive guide on how to use Mount Kenya University student portal

May 09, 2024
Post

Mount Kenya University (MKU) is one of the best private schools in Kenya. It has many branches in the country making it accessible to as many people as possible. Its programmes are also relatively cheaper, making it the choice of many learners. Mount Kenya University courses are also accredited by the commission for university education. With an increase in enrollment rates, the school needs to have an information management system to handle its operations efficiently. All this brought about the creation of Mount Kenya University student portal.

You have been admitted to Mount Kenya University as a student. Where do you begin? As a student, you will require several services from the university. To access those services, you have to create your account using the school’s website.

Have you ever wanted a fee structure but had to go through a long process before you could get one? Using the portal, you can access services such as fee statements, exam timetables, results, unit registration, booking of hostels, and so much more without having to engage in the old office bureaucracies.

Services offered by Mount Kenya student portal

Many services can be accessed from the MKU university student online portal without having to visit any office. Some of the things that can be found online include the MKU student portal graduation list. Such lists can be accessed quickly and improve the learner experience. Here is a complete list of the services that can be accessed with the help of the page:

  • Information updates: Sometimes, universities have brochures and prospectus that are not regularly updated. The portal allows the university to update information in real-time, thus increasing efficiency.
  • Requesting of learner identification card: Without the scholar identification card, learners may not be able to access essential services and facilities. Learners can request for an ID to be processed from their portal.
  • Fee statements: Through the portal, learners can access the right fees structures tailored to their courses, year and semester of study, thus avoiding unnecessary confusion.
  • Semester timetables: A timetable is placed on the page to show the courses offered in a particular semester, the lecturers to teach those courses and time in which they will be taught.
  • Course registration: Using the portal students can register for the correct courses available for each semester.
  • Exam results enquiries: The page has a feature for scholars to request for their exam results.
  • Requesting of result slips and printing of transcripts.
  • Hostel booking.

Downloading admission letters for new scholars

After filling Mount Kenya University application forms, prospective scholars are expected to follow up with ensuring they meet all the admission requirements. New scholars joining Mount Kenya University are expected to download their admission letters from the portal. Here, the list of scholars admitted and their prospective courses can also be accessed. The procedure for downloading the admission letter is as follows:

  • Visit the student portal page.
  • Go to the applicants/new students section.
  • Enter the application reference number or the candidate’s index number.
  • Click on generate applicant letter of offer.

    Creating a student account

    Here is how to create a scholar’s account:

  • Visit the official university website.
  • Look for the student menu and click on it.
  • From there, select the student portal.
  • You will be redirected to the Mount Kenya University student portal page.
  • Look for the login section.
  • Use your admission number and ID number to create your account.

    How to login to Mount Kenya University student portal

    The process to login to the page is easy and straight forward from the university website. Follow the following simple procedure to log in to the page:

  • Once in the site type your registration number. Ensure the registration number is written in full, including the year. For example, instead of /19 the year should be /2019.
  • The initial password for your portal account is your national ID number/ passport number or mobile phone number without any spaces.
  • Click on login to access your account.

    • If you have forgotten your login password all you have to do is click on the change password section, key in your student registration number and your password will be reset after the portal administrator has updated the information.

    Mount Kenya University has invested a lot into the ICT department to ensure students get the best experience. With the Mount Kenya University student portal, learners can access almost all services from their computers or mobile phones. It begins with the letter of offer that can be generated simply by inputting the reference number given during application. With the distance digital learning programme, learners will be thankful for having the student portal. It will help them manage how they interact with the school.
