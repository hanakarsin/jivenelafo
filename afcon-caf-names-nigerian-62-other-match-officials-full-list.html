CAF Names Nigerian, 62 Other Match Officials [Full List]

June 13, 2024
Post

The Confederation of African Football (CAF) has announced a list of 63 match officials to officiate at the 2021 Africa Cup of Nations (AFCON) holding in Cameroon.

Naija News reports that the list released by CAF on Tuesday consists of 24 referees, 31 assistant referees and eight video assistant referees (VARs) from 36 countries.

Samuel Pwadutakam, an assistant referee, is the only Nigerian official listed for the tournament sent to kick off on January 9 and end on February 6.

All the officials would complete their final preparations in Cameroon the week before the opening game.
