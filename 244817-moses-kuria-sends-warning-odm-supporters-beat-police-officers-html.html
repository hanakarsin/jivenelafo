Moses Kuria sends warning to ODM supporters after they beat up police officers

July 29, 2024
Post

- Gatundu South MP Moses Kuria has vowed to beat up ODM supporters in Siaya

- This is after the youths from the party beat up police officers who were protecting supporters of Rarieda MP Nicholas Gumbo

- An angry Kuria threatened to remove the intestines of the ODM supporters and throw it in Lake Victoria

Gatundu South MP Moses Kuria has vowed he will beat up Orange Democratic Movement (ODM) supporters in Siaya county.

This is after the youths allied to the party brutally beat up police officers shielding supporters of Rarieda MP Nicholas Gumbo.

Kuria in a post seen by TUKO.co.ke, said he would have dealt seriously with youths if he was there.

Kuria said Gumbo was a kind man, but if it was him or his supporters being attacked by the youths, he would have beaten them up and called the county workers to sweep away their intestines and throw it in Lake Victoria.

The controversial legislator called on Interior Cabinet Secretary Joseph Nkaissery and the Inspector General of Police to take action against the youths.

''Joseph Nkaissery, IG Boinett these are ODM goons attacking police officers in Siaya. The officers were shielding Gumbo's supporters from the goons. Gumbo is a kind man,

''I am not. They try such stuff on me and you will send county workers to sweep their intestines and throw them to Lake Victoria,'' Kuria said.

The youths had turned against Gumbo and his supporters after he had earlier resigned from ODM and became an independent candidate.
