  • Jemutai shared screenshots of the fake account and an M-Pesa transaction confirmation revealing the person's name

Jemutai exposed a Facebook account with username, Jemutai Churchill and operated as hers.

The mother of two divulged that the account has been conning innocent Kenyans who believe that she is the owner of the account.

The account has about 5Ok followers and is linked to a number that Jemutai found out to have been registered under M-Pesa name Bylon Rondi.

The funny comedienne of Churchill Show said she has warned the impersonator many times to no avail.

She added that the person also posts sensitive stuff harmful to her reputation.

This is what she wrote:

"The guy running the fake Jemutai Churchill account is called Bylon Rondi. I have got all his numbers. I have asked him severally to stop but he continues to use the account to con innocent Kenyans and post some sensitive posts using my images. It's just a matter of time."
