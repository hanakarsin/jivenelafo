The General Overseer of the Mountain of Liberation and Miracles Ministries, a.k.a Liberation City, Dr. Chris Okafor, is popularly known for his unrivalled prophetic accuracy.

In 2014, he accurately prophesied the expiry date for Ebola in Nigeria and it happened just as he had said.

He also prophesied in 2013, that former President Goodluck Jonathan was going to win elections in 2015 and much later in 2014, he prophesied that the hand of God had left the former president and that there was no need for him to re-contest.
